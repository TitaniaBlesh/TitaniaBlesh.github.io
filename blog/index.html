Writing notes

Writing is not about inspiration.
Writing is not about ideas.
Writing is not about luck.
Writing is about skill.

· Brandon Sanderson

I used to believe writing was an innate talent, a calling, the touch of inspiration. None of which I have.

Writing is much more than that: it‘s study, dedication, persistence, willpower. It‘s following rules. When I started to study, everything changed. I found a publisher. Then another.

This blog is my archive of everything I have learned about writing, from craft basics to my own experiences (and misadventures). A place to keep my notes in one ordered collection, and to share them with whoever wants to read along and learn day by day.
