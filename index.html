The author

Hey, I'm Tita.

I write sci-fi and fantasy for adults and young adults. I'm traditionally published in Italy, France, and Canada – and also on Proxima Centauri b, though you'll know for sure only in 4.25 light-years.

I love astronomy, volcanoes, handstands, and my dog Newt. My life goal is to climb every volcano on Earth and Olympus Mons on Mars.
